Visual Appeal: Use high-quality images and videos to showcase products. Apple places a strong emphasis on visual appeal, so ensure your visuals are compelling and reflect the premium quality of Apple products. Responsive Design: Given the diversity of devices visitors might use (from iPhones to MacBooks), ensure your store is fully responsive.
